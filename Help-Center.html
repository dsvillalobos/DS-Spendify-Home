What's DS Spendify?

DS Spendify is an expense tracking app designed to simplify your financial management. With its intuitive interface and smart features, you can effortlessly track your expenses, set budgets, and receive insightful reports to take control of your spending habits.

How to Install DS Spendify?

iOS

  1. 1. Tap the Share icon at the bottom of the Safari browser window. It resembles a square with an arrow pointing upwards.
  2. 2. In the Share menu, scroll down and locate the "Add to Home Screen" option. Tap on it.
  3. 3. On the "Add to Home" screen, tap the "Add" button located in the top-right corner.
  4. 4. The "DS Spendify" App will be added to your device's home screen.
  5. 5. You can now access the "DS Spendify" App.

Android

  1. 1. Tap the three-dot menu icon in the top-right corner of the Chrome browser window.
  2. 2. In the menu that appears, look for the "Add to Home screen" option and tap on it.
  3. 3. A dialog box will appear. Tap the "Add" button.
  4. 4. The "DS Spendify" App will be added to your device's home screen.
  5. 5. You can now access the "DS Spendify" App.

Windows and macOS

  1. 1. On your computer, open Chrome.
  2. 2. At the top right of the address bar, click Install

How to Create my Profile?

  1. 1. On DS Spendify, go to the "Profile" page.
  2. 2. Add your Username, your Income and your Saving Target.
  3. 3. Click on "Save Profile".

How to Add Expenses?

  1. 1. On DS Spendify, go to the category you want to add an expense to.
  2. 2. Add the Expense Name, its Date and its Amount ($).
  3. 3. Click on "Add Expense".

What are "Financial Personalities"?

"Financial Personalities" refer to the different characteristics, attitudes, and behaviors individuals exhibit in managing their finances.
